How to Play

Overview

Booray is a trick-taking card game where players compete to win the pot. But beware - if you take zero tricks, you get "Booray'd" and must match the pot!

Setup

  • 2-6 players, standard 52-card deck
  • Each round, all players ante into the pot
  • Each player is dealt 5 cards
  • The dealer's 5th card determines the trump suit

Declare Phase

  • Starting left of dealer, each player declares: Play or Fold
  • Players who fold lose their ante but risk nothing more
  • Players who play may discard 0-5 cards and draw replacements

Playing Tricks

  • 5 tricks are played per round
  • You MUST follow suit if possible
  • If void in led suit, you MUST play trump if possible
  • When trumping, you must try to beat the highest trump played
  • If void in both led suit and trump, play any card
  • Highest card of led suit wins (unless trumped)

Card Ranking

A (high) K Q J 10 9 8 7 6 5 4 3 2 (low). Trump suit beats all non-trump cards.

Winning

  • Player with the most tricks wins the entire pot
  • TIE: If players tie for most tricks, the pot carries over
  • BOORAY: Any player who played but took ZERO tricks must match the pot for the next round

Game Over

The game ends when a player runs out of chips (reaches 0 or below).
